# AM-Transmitter-and-Reciever
Group Final Project with John Danison and Matt Tolla pushing our knowlege of receiving data to the other side

AM Transmitter (ADALM-PLUTO) + Receiver (RTL SDR) Radio Station
Used:
  Online ADALM Pluto guide for Matlab Simulink setup
  ECET 349 Lab 5 audio equalizer code from class
  Lecture slides for AM transmitter/receiver
Challenges:
  Finding suitable audio source -> Mono music sampled at 8kHz
  Used online converters for mp3 files of our favorite songs
Why do it?
  Learned how to make a fully legal AM radio within the Family Radio Service (FRS) range using a new type of SDR
  Can be applied to transmit and receive weather reports, person to person communication, & emergency situations
